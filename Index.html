My Experiences

During my internship at Verizon Communications in 2023, I created advanced NLP techniques using R programming, generating topic models from freeform text data. These models became instrumental in highlighting key trends, particularly in the realm of risk management, where I was crucial to reshaping Verizon's audit approach for a more holistic management of both immediate and long-term risks.

At Capital One in 2022, my focus shifted towards the intricate challenges of data privacy. I critically assessed the implications of sharing customer financial data with third-party entities, developing a rigorous protocol to ensure compliance with data privacy regulations. My proficiency with Python and its analytical libraries also facilitated the creation of many visual tools such as line and bar graphs, highlighting historical data breaches within Capital One.

While with Planet Cents, Inc. in 2021, I delved deeply into the software development lifecycle, architecting key website features that bolstered user engagement. Collaborating within a team framework, I was exposed to agile methodologies, ensuring rapid, iterative development and fostering consistent UI experiences across diverse platforms and devices.
